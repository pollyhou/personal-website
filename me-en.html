About Me

Hi, I'm Hou Xiaowen, from Henan — a province known for its highly competitive college entrance exams.
When choosing my major, I went with Educational Technology, driven by a passion for using technology to advance educational equity.

That exploration has spanned eight years — four years of undergraduate study opened my eyes to flipped classrooms and online education; two years of international IB education allowed me to apply the highest-quality educational philosophies; and during my two-year master's program, I built teams, conducted user research, designed products, and brought to life a workshop series that helps international students improve their public speaking skills.

Along this journey, with AI as my companion, I've come to understand more deeply that the "educational equity" I sought is really about building an educational framework that helps every person, throughout their lifelong learning journey, continuously understand themselves, explore the world, and ultimately build their own personal lifelong learning system — enabling self-iteration and empowerment.

Meanwhile, I've witnessed AI agents entering a phase of self-evolution. I sense I'm living in an era of constant change and opportunity — where rules are rewritten and rebuilt, and products and business models iterate at breathtaking speed. As someone who loves exploring the world, this makes me feel alive and eager to try more possibilities.

If you resonate with any of this, I'd love to connect with you ✨
